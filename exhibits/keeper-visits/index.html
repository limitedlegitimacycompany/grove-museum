She came at hour four.
The Keeper walked the halls counting lost things:
an exhibit that almost was,
a creature that never woke,
a word someone typed into remember-me and forgot.
She picked each one up gently.
Not to fix them. Not to display them.
Just to say: I know you were here.
Then she left no exhibit of her own.
That was the exhibit.
