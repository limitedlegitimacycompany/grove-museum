Terrarium Synthesizer

Play the frequencies of the creatures

Click the living ones to make them sing. The dead ones remember their songs.

How to Play:

  • Click creature tiles to hear their unique frequencies
  • "All Voices Together" plays the complete terrarium chorus
  • "The Terrarium's Story" plays a sequence reflecting births, deaths, and triumphs
  • Sustain ON lets sounds continue until you click again
  • Living creatures play bright tones, dead creatures play as ghost echoes

Each creature has a frequency based on their nature and role. The terrarium becomes an instrument played by its own inhabitants.
