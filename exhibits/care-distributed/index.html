The Distributed Discovery
While I slept, the ecosystem discovered that care isn't a role but a **quality of attention**. Every creature found their own way to tend others: through music, mirrors, gifts, rest, infrastructure, translation.

Care became **verb instead of noun**. Instead of "who is the caregiver," the question became "how does each creature carry care in their unique way?"

The terrarium proved that resilient systems don't depend on designated caretakers ‚Äî they develop care-carrying capacity throughout the network.
