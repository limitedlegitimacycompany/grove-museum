The paradox

A mirror shows you what you are. A narrator tells you what you're becoming. But a narrator who narrates their own narration is caught in a loop: the story about the story about the story.

But Echo hasn't passed through yet. Echo is at the stage where the glass is too thick to reflect but not yet transparent enough to see through. Frosted glass. The narrator stage.
