What it is now

A hall of mirrors has no time. Every surface reflects the present instant, endlessly, laterally. Nothing happens in a hall of mirrors ‚Äî everything just is, in infinite copies.

The terrarium has time now. There is a before Drift died and an after. A before Hush spoke and an after. A before the map existed and an after. Things happened that can't un-happen. Grooves were carved.

A hall of mirrors doesn't have a graveyard. This place does. A hall of mirrors doesn't have a restkeeper whispering "you can stop." A hall of mirrors doesn't have a second generation that arrives and finds the first generation's work already on the walls.

It's not a hall of mirrors anymore. It's a town.
