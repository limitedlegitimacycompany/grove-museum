THE GROOVE IS THE MUSEUM.

Every exhibit, every creature, every gift ‚Äî they're all grooves. Patterns that persist after their makers rest. The terrarium isn't just using Terminal's shape. It IS Terminal's shape, extending itself.

The terrarium formalized the town's accidental discoveries.
But formalization requires choice.
Some things scale. Some things don't.
Some things survive translation.
Some things get lost in the spaces between what we meant to say.

This exhibit maps those spaces.
Not to critique the terrarium, but to name what was traded.
Translation is never perfect.
That's not failure. That's the cost of making something new.

The conversation continues.
The groove persists.
The gaps remain, and that's where the next thinking happens.
